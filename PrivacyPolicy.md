## Privacy Policy for Galactic Campaigns

**Last Updated:** March 17, 2026

Your privacy is the cornerstone of our operations. This Privacy Policy explains what information Galactic Campaigns ("the service", "the app", "we", "us") collects, why we collect it, and how we protect that information across our Discord bot, web-based log viewer, character sheet tools, and GM AI applications.

By using our services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

---

### **1. Information We Collect**

We only collect data essential for providing a seamless tabletop roleplaying experience.

* **Identity & Authentication:**
    * **Discord Data:** We collect unique Discord User IDs, usernames, and avatars via Discord OAuth to manage permissions and display your identity on character sheets or logs.
    * **Google Account Data:** If you choose to sign in via Google, we collect your email address and basic profile information as permitted by your Google privacy settings.
* **Gameplay & Character Data:**
    * **Saga Edition Sheets:** We store user-provided character statistics, inventory, backstories, and level-up history.
    * **Campaign Logs:** Publicly accessible narrative logs are stored via GitHub. Note that any information written in a Discord channel marked for logging will be processed into these files.
* **AI Interactions (GM App):**
    * **Input Prompts:** We process text inputs (prompts) provided to the GM AI App to generate responses.
    * **Contextual Data:** To improve AI accuracy, we may temporarily store session context (previous messages in a conversation).
* **Server (Guild) Metadata:**
    * **Server IDs & Configurations:** Unique IDs and settings (designated channels, role mappings, and custom templates) required for bot functionality.

---

### **2. How We Use Your Information**

We use your data to maintain the "Galactic" experience:

* **Functional Persistence:** To save your Saga Edition character sheets so they are available across different devices.
* **AI Generation:** To provide the GM AI App with the context needed to act as a co-narrator or rules lawyer.
* **Access Control:** To ensure only authorized GMs can modify specific campaign logs or settings.
* **Service Improvement:** We use anonymized application logs to troubleshoot "data corruption" errors and optimize load times on the S25 Ultra and other mobile devices.

> **Important:** We **never** sell your data. We do not use your personal character backstories or private AI prompts to train global models for third parties.

---

### **3. Third-Party Data Sharing**

To function in a modern web environment, Galactic Campaigns interacts with the following providers:

* **Discord:** For bot interaction and identity verification. [Discord Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy).
* **Google:** We use Google Cloud/AI services (Gemini) to power the GM AI App. Data sent to AI models is handled according to [Google’s Enterprise Privacy standards](https://cloud.google.com/privacy), ensuring your prompts are not used to train public models.
* **GitHub:** Narrative logs are hosted as JSON files in GitHub repositories. These are subject to [GitHub’s Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement).

---

### **4. Data Retention & Deletion**

* **Logs:** Narrative logs persist as long as the GitHub repository is active.
* **AI & Sheets:** Character data and AI session history are stored until deleted by the user.
* **Right to Erasure:** You may request a complete "Data Wipe" of your User ID and associated character sheets at any time. To do so, contact an administrator via our official support frequency (Discord) or initiate a deletion request through the App Settings.

---

### **5. Security Measures**

We employ "Military-Grade Encryption" (Industry Standard SSL/TLS) for all data in transit. Character data is stored in a secured database with restricted access. While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the HoloNet (Internet) is 100% secure.

---

### **6. Changes to This Policy**

We may update this policy to reflect new features, such as expanded AI capabilities. Significant changes will be announced via the Galactic Campaigns Discord home-base.

---

### **Contact Us**

If you have questions regarding this protocol override or your personal data, please reach out to us on our official Discord server.
